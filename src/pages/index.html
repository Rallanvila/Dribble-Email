The Weekend Lineup

Spend the next few days getting cozy on the couch. We’ve got your weekend plans right here.

Friday Nightss

GENERA+ION

Spend Friday night catching up on the first three episodes of the new Max Original, Genera+ion. A group of Orange County high schoolers navigate sexuality and identity, testing the beliefs of their conservative community.

Saturday Afternoon

SOUTH PARQ
VACCINATION SPECIAL

Spend Saturday with your favorite troublemakers. Come on down to South ParQ for a brand new special. While the adults clamor for the COVID-19 vaccine, a hilarious militant group tries to stop the boys from getting their teacher vaccinated.

Saturday Night

JUDAS AND THE
BLACK MESSIAH

Settle in for a Saturday night movie, and don’t miss your chance to see this award-winning drama. Daniel Kaluuya and LaKeith Stanfield star in the new movie inspired by true events. Now in theaters and on HBO Max. Streaming through March 14.

Sunday Afternoon

ALLEN V. FARROW

Catch up on Allen v. Farrow before the finale airs this Sunday. This HBO Original docuseries pieces together new perspectives and details about the abuse scandal and custody battle between Woody Allen and Mia Farrow - and the controversial aftermath.

Sunday Night

LAST WEEK TONIGHT WITH
JOHN OLIVER

End your weekend Max marathon with a healthy dose of dry wit and British exasperation as John Oliver delivers your weekly news briefing. New episodes air Sunday nights at 11 pm ET.
